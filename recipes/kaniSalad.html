Kani Salad

Description

Kani Salad is a refershing japanese dish taht usees imitation crab together with cucumber, spicy mayonnaise dressing to make a delicious salad.

Ingredients

  • Imitation Crab
  • Spicy Mayonnaise
  • Cucumber
  • Masago (Optional)
  • Seseme (Optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, break up the imitation crab into strands
  2. Add the cut cucumbers
  3. Add the mayonnaise
  4. Add the optional Ingredients
  5. Mix
